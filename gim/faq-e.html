  • General

  • About GIM?

    Meet GIM, the new, revolutionary app that gives you safe, secure communication and digital freedom in the mass exodus from Big Tech. Subscribe official Gettr account: https://www.gettr.com/user/oasistech。

  • How to Register and Login?

    New users can register a new account through the register button at the bottom right corner of the mobile APP. Registration does not require any personal information. Just set a custom account and password to successfully register and log in.

  • How to log out?

    After successfully logging in to the APP, click "My"> "User Settings"> "Logout" to log out.

  • What is displayed after successful login?

    After the login is successful, the dialogue page is displayed, showing a list of all chat rooms, there are two or more chat rooms. Swipe to the left after selecting a chat room, and shortcut operations such as "mute, top, delete" appear. Note: "Delete" on the conversation page is equivalent to deleting the friend and all previous chat messages, but the deleted friend will still be displayed in the address book list, but it is no longer a friend.

  • Can I delete the message I sent?

    You can completely delete your chat content, whether you are in a private chat or a group chat, you can delete the information you posted at any time, and there is no time limit.

  • APP

  • What Mobile phone systems are supported by GIM?

    Currently, it is recommended that users use devices that support the following system versions: Android runs on OS8.0 or later; iPhone runs on iOS13 or later.

  • How to download and install the GIM?

    GIM is available on the Apple market and Google market. Log in to the APP market and search for GIM to download and install it. Android devices can also be installed directly through the APK installation package. The download address provided on the website is the official release address, and it is not recommended to download and install it through other addresses. Click Download

  • How do I update my GIM mobile app?

    When a new version of GIM is updated, Apple Market and Google Market will automatically remind you that there is a new version update, and you can install the update as prompted.

  • Account

  • I forgot my login password, can I retrieve it?

    Currently GIM does not support the account retrieval function. If you forget your password, please re-register a new account and log in.

  • How to change my password?

    After the login is successful, you can change the password in "My"> "My Information", please remember the password you changed.

  • Can I delete my account?

    At present, GIM does not support the function of deleting accounts by itself.

  • Operation

  • How to find and add friends?

    Click the "+" sign in the upper right corner of the APP, you can add friends by nickname, or you can scan your friends' personal QR codes to add friends.

  • How to enter the chat conversation?

    When adding a friend for the first time, it will automatically appear on the "Conversation" page. You can also open the "Contacts" page, find the friend and click the "Start Chat" button to enter the chat room to start chatting.

  • What message types are supported by GIM chat?

    GIM chat currently supports the sending, receiving, and playing of text message sessions, voice message sessions, GIF animation, pictures, videos, and document files, and the recording, sending, receiving, playback, and speed change of voice messages in the session window.

  • Does GIM support voice and video calls?

    Currently, only voice message sending is supported. Voice calls and video calls are already in the version development plan, please be patient.

  • How to create a group?

    Click "+" in the upper right corner of the APP, select "Create Group", you can customize the group name, topic, whether to encrypt, whether to public and other attributes. Private groups: Private groups are encrypted by default, and users can only join when they are invited. Public group: Public groups are divided into encrypted and non-encrypted. If you choose to encrypt, you cannot disable it after it is turned on. Public searchable group: This group can be searched in "Join Group", and you can join freely. Click the three dots in the upper right corner of the newly created group chat room window to enter the group settings, modify the chat room icon, name, view member information, etc., and set member permissions and add and delete on the group member page , Prohibit members and other operations.

  • What is the function of Contacts?

    1. You can check the "friend request" in the communication to join and agree or reject it, and accept or reject it in "Contacts"> "Friend Request". 2. View or search the established group information in the list of My Groups in the address book of the iOS terminal.

  • What does My Page do?

    1. You can modify personal information in "My Page", such as nickname, avatar, password and other information. 2. User preferences can be set, such as message reminder settings, theme style settings, key import and export functions, and account logout functions.

  • Security

  • Regarding the security issues of Apple ID downloading and using GIM in mainland China?

    Gim will not actively save the key and data in iCloud unless the user synchronizes the mobile phone data to the mainland's iCloud, so there is a risk that the mainland government will review the data in iCloud. Gim's key is stored in the user's own mobile phone. Here, other data is stored on GIM's overseas servers. Therefore, we suggest that it is best not to download and use Gim with Apple IDs in Mainland China and Hong Kong. If you have to use it, please turn off the iCloud sync backup function.

  • All chats are encrypted?

    Private chats are encrypted one-to-one by default. Only group chats can be set to be encrypted when creating a group, but it cannot be disabled after encryption is turned on.

  • What does import and export keys mean?

    Exporting the key is equivalent to backing up user information and keys. Importing the key can restore chat information when logging in to the account again or transferring the device.

  • What should I do if my account is stolen?

    It is a pity that your account has been maliciously stolen. This situation is likely to happen. Generally, this situation occurs because the user’s password is too simple and was guessed by people with ulterior motives. But please rest assured that even if your account is stolen, you The information of is also safe, no one will see your information. But please be sure to set a more complicated password and keep it in mind.

  • Feedback

  • What to do if the message sent by a friend or group member is not decrypted?

    1. You can export the key in "My"> "User Settings"> "Export Key", exit gim, log in again, and perform the above steps "Import Key". 2. iOS users can try "My"> "User Settings"> "Clear Cache".

  • How to check your own GIM version information?

    Open the APP, click "My"> "User Settings"> "About GIM", you can view the current version number of GIM.
